My name is Pedro Benedetti, but you can call me Shiro (from 46neko [white cat], 46 = しろ, a little joke with the readings of the numbers) or Pepo (an old high school nickname). I am a PhD student at Kumamoto University and a member of Aritsugi Lab (also known as DBMS). よろしくぞ!

I also hope to revisit video game research (if my advisors permits!).
